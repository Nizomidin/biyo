# How to Edit Patient Status and Price/Balance

## Editing Patient Status and Balance

### Step 1: Open Patient Card
1. Go to the **Patients** page
2. Click on any patient from the list to open their patient card

### Step 2: Enter Edit Mode
- Click the **"Редактировать"** (Edit) button at the top right of the patient card
- The card will enter edit mode, showing input fields instead of text

### Step 3: Edit Status
1. Find the **"Статус"** (Status) field in the patient information section
2. Click on the dropdown to see available status options:
   - **Активный** (Active)
   - **На лечении** (In Treatment)
   - **Вылечен** (Got Well)
   - **Неактивный** (Inactive)
   - **Переведен** (Transferred)
3. Select the desired status

### Step 4: Edit Balance/Price
1. Find the **"Баланс (смн)"** (Balance) field in the patient information section
2. Enter the new balance amount
3. You can enter positive numbers (debt) or negative numbers (credit/overpayment)

### Step 5: Save Changes
- Click the **"Сохранить"** (Save) button at the top right
- Or click **"Отмена"** (Cancel) to discard changes

## Quick Tips

- **Status colors**: When viewing (not editing), the status is displayed as text. When editing, use the dropdown.
- **Balance**: The balance field supports decimal values (e.g., 150.50)
- **Auto-calculation**: The balance can also be calculated automatically from visits, but you can manually override it
- **Multiple edits**: You can edit status, balance, and other fields all at once before saving
